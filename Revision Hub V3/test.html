Exam Reminders

check_circle

Key Dos

  • • Show method: Missing steps loses potential marks even if final values are wrong.
  • • Read fully: Check the question carefully to you know what is required.
  • • Equipment: Ensure you have your ruler, pen, pencil and scientific calculator.
cancel

Key Don'ts

  • • Don't stall: Don't stay on the same question for ages, move on and circle back.
  • • No blanks: Unanswered questions are guaranteed zero. Ensure you have a go!
  • • Don't cram: Your brain is a muscle and needs time to recover.
