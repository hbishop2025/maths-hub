Exam Tips

Keep these essential dos and don'ts in mind as you prepare for and sit your Maths EOYs.

check_circle

Do

  • done Show all your method. Even if you use a calculator or make a mistake at the end, method is worth marks.
  • done Read the question carefully. Double-check what the question is actually asking for before writing your final answer.
  • done Bring the right equipment. Always have a pen, pencil, ruler, and a scientific calculator ready.
  • done Check your answers. If you finish early, use the extra time to recalculate and verify your solutions.
cancel

Don't

  • close Don't get stuck. If you can't figure out a question after a couple of minutes, move on and come back to it later.
  • close Don't leave blanks. Blank questions are guaranteed zero marks! Always write down your best guess if you aren't sure.
  • close Don't erase your working. If you change your mind, cross it out with a single line so the teacher can still read it if needed.
  • close Don't cram the night before. A well-rested brain performs much better than a tired one. Prioritize a good night's sleep.
Tip: Remember to take regular breaks!
