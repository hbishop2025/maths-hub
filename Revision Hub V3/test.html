config_tutor_mode.json
Act as a maths tutor.
I am stuck on [Topic Name].
Explain the method step-by-step, but do not give me the final answer yet. Ask me a guiding question to help me solve it myself.
config_quiz_gen.json
Generate 5 practice questions on [Topic] suitable for [Year Group].
Order them by difficulty (Easy -> Hard).
Provide the answers in a hidden dropdown or at the very bottom of the response.
ks3_fractions.exe
I am in Year 7. Explain how to add fractions with different denominators.
Use a real-life example involving pizza or chocolate.
Then give me 3 practice questions.
ks3_ratios.exe
Create a ratio word problem suitable for Year 8.
Show me the Bar Model method to solve it.
gcse_exam_sim.exe
Give me a Grade 7 question on Circle Theorems.
Wait for my answer.
If I get it wrong, tell me which theorem I missed, rather than just giving the solution.
gcse_mark_scheme.exe
Here is my working out for a question on Simultaneous Equations:
[PASTE YOUR WORKING]
Mark this out of 3 marks. Highlighting exactly where I lost marks.
alevel_stats.py

Explain the difference between a one-tailed and two-tailed test using a context about manufacturing bolts.
Show how to write the hypotheses H0 and H1.
alevel_pure.py
Generate a hard integration by parts question involving ln(x).
Provide a hint about which term to set as 'u' and which as 'dv/dx', but don't solve it yet.
