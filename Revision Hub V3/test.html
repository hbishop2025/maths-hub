Safety & Academic Integrity

check_circle

Always double-check calculations

AI can sometimes hallucinate arithmetic. Use it for the "how" and "why," but verify the final answer.

check_circle

Use AI for personalized tutoring

Ask the AI to explain a topic in five different ways until one clicks for you.

cancel

Never input personal data

Don't provide names, addresses, or school IDs. Keep conversations strictly about maths.

Avoid "Copy-Paste" Homework
