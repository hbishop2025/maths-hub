About this revision hub

This site pulls together worksheets, videos and quick links for SJWMS maths students. Start with the smart search above if you already know the topic, or use the course hubs (KS3, GCSE, A-Level) to browse by year group and unit.

At the moment, the hub is focused on Year 9 and A-Level (Year 12 & Year 13). Other year groups will be added and polished over time.

How to use this site effectively

1 Search by topic

Use the smart search above to type in a topic, chapter name or key word (e.g. “Ch3 quadratics” or “simultaneous equations”).

2 Open the hub or slides

Results will take you either to this revision hub (worksheets & videos) or straight to our A-level Google Sites pages.

3 Practice on the platforms

Use the links below (Dr Frost, SPARX, Pearson ActiveLearn, MyMaths) to complete independent practice and homework.
