Privacy & Policy

Our Commitment

This Maths Hub is a revision platform for students. We keep things simple and private:

  • No personal data is collected from students or teachers.
  • No logins or accounts are required to access resources.
  • Resources are linked from trusted, teacher-approved sources only.
  • We comply with GDPR because no identifying data is processed or stored.

Questions?

If you have any questions about how this site works, please speak to the Maths Hub website administrator.
