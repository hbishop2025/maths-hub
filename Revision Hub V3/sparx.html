Your Weekly Task

Complete the Compulsory Sparx Task, making sure you write down the bookwork code, working out, and answer in the back of your orange book. Your working will be checked after the deadline, and failure to produce the required working in your orange book will result in a detention being set.

info

If you have any issues accessing or completing your SPARX homework, please speak to your maths teacher before the Wednesday deadline so they can help you avoid a missed task.
