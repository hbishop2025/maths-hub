Quick How-To Cards

Expanding
How do I expand \( (x+3)(x-5) \)?
Steps
  1. FOIL: \(x\times x=x^2\)
  2. Outer: \(x\times(-5)=-5x\)
  3. Inner: \(3\times x=3x\)
  4. Last: \(3\times(-5)=-15\)
  5. Collect: \(x^2-2x-15\)
Factorising
How do I factorise \( x^2-7x+12 \)?
Steps
  1. Find two numbers → product \(=+12\), sum \(=-7\): \(-3\) and \(-4\).
  2. Answer: \((x-3)(x-4)\).
Solving
How do I solve \( x^2-5x-6=0 \)?
Steps
  1. Factorise: \(x^2-5x-6=(x-6)(x+1)\).
  2. Set factors \(=0\): \(x-6=0\) or \(x+1=0\).
  3. Solutions: \(x=6\) or \(x=-1\).
Sequences
How do I spot a quadratic sequence?
Steps
  1. Find first differences.
  2. If first differences aren’t constant, find second differences.
  3. Constant second difference ⇒ quadratic sequence.
